At Rich Architects, we pride ourselves in designing innovative, sustainable buildings. We are passionate about Architecture and Africa and strive to find sustainable building solutions suitable for Africa in the 21st century. We are a 10-people strong Johannesburg based architectural team that satellites’ to the award winning office, Peter Rich Architects, which currently has projects on the go in South Africa as well as Kigali, Rwanda, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, Ngorongoro Crater, Tanzania, Chicago, USA and Anji, Zhejiang Province, China.

2016 has proven to be a very exciting year where we were involved in a wide variety of projects, ranging from a 6 storey 95-unit upmarket apartment block in Rivonia, Johannesburg to a 200-unit resort that makes use of natural stone and earth-vaulted structures in the bamboo forests of Anji, Zhejiang Province, China.

2017 marks exciting times ahead, as we move into our new offices in 4th Avenue, Melville. We welcome the new opportunities that our new offices bring.

No job is too big or too small. Residential houses, holiday houses, clinics, schools to lodges in exotic destinations; we can design any building type and we recognise that every project has its own set of unique challenges.

Through small changes we can make a big difference!!!
